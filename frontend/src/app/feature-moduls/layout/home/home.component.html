THE PSYCHOLOGICAL CENTER

Welcome! The Psychological Center is a community mental health clinic located in Novi Sad, on the campus of City College. Our clinic offers a variety of services to cater to the needs of adults, adolescents, and children. Here we believe in a collaborative and supportive therapeutic relationship.
